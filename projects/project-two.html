3D Holographic Fan

Spinning LED fan projecting floating images on demand.

Arduino3D PrintingElectronic CircuitsLaser CuttingSolidworks

For my final Electrical Engineering project, we were given creative freedom to build anything within the scope of the course. The inspiration struck the night before while at KungFu Tea,where I noticed a mesmerizing floating image of bubble tea projected into mid-air. I instantly knew I wanted to recreate that effect from scratch.

I designed the fan’s mechanical structure in SolidWorks, incorporating a battery mount directly onto the rotating shaft to eliminate wire tangling. From scratch, I wrote the code that allowed any image to be uploaded and automatically mapped onto a 1D LED strip. The program calculated precise RGB values based on the rod’s rotation angle and speed, enabling real-time persistence-of-vision image rendering. This project pulled together mechanical design, embedded programming, and signal timing—and left me with a deep sense of creative and technical fulfillment.
